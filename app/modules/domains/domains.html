Domain Addons

Registration Details

By clicking Register Domain you agree to pay for a 1 year registration. Your credit card will be charged immediately.

Your domain will be registered through Enom, an ICANN accredited domain registrar.

By registering this domain you agree to the DNSimple Domain Registration Agreement and the Enom Domain Registration Agreement.

Transfer Details

  • Make sure to disable WHOIS privacy protection if you have enabled it BEFORE you submit your transfer order, otherwise your transfer will likely fail!
  • If you are not already using DNSimple's name servers and you would like to then set up and test your DNS records from DNSimple before you move the domain. Add the domain to your account with "Use domain services" in the Add domain page.
  • Transfers are typically disallowed for domains registered within the last 60 days.
  • Please unlock the domain in the existing registrar before submitting your transfer request.

Before you start

To use our DNS service, you'll have to update your current name servers at your registrar.
